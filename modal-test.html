An island for those looking for something exciting, wild, something remote or even relaxing

Achill Island has kayaking, surfing, cycling and hiking for those who want the thrills and spas and relaxation spaces for those who want to chill!

Keem Beach is arguably one of the most picturesque places in Ireland and is not to be missed

Strandhill

Another hidden gem on the west coast of Ireland.

In Strandhill you could hop off a surfboard and slip in to a seaweed bath in a matter of minutes. If you need to cool down, head to a cafe for some delicious homemade ice cream.

From Strandhill, it's easy to reach Mullaghmore - one of the most famous surfing locations in the world!

GAA

Did you know Ireland is home to two unique sports that outdate sports such as soccer, American football, baseball, basketball and ice hockey?

These two sports are called Gaelic football (which inspired Aussie Rules football) and hurling (which inspired ice hockey)

These sports are fast paced, physically challenging and highly entertaining sports played in front of small or huge crowds (up to 80,000) and those that play are all amateur athletes. Check them out below
